Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer worldwide shipping with expedited and standard options. All shipments are fully insured and require signature upon delivery.

Yes, we specialize in custom jewelry design. Please contact our design team to schedule a consultation or discuss your vision.

Can I schedule an appointment to view specific pieces?

Absolutely. We offer private showings by appointment. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to schedule your visit.
